Welcome to Cardinal Insights

Cardinal provides the evidence base that lets our customers act with confidence. Human intelligence is central to our work. We turn information into actionable insight by harnessing original thought, deep topic knowledge, and real-world practical experience. This intelligence is often unpublished and otherwise inaccessible. It comes from our network of Expert Associates, and our ability to identify and engage with Subject Matter Experts. Our work informs government departments, regulators, and security & policing.

Our Services

  • Problem definition, research design, and delivery team assembly
  • Secondary research. Including AI discovery, market assessment, OSINT, and academic and grey literature reviews
  • Qualitative primary research. Iterative depth interviews with identified independent experts with specific experience of topics, companies, or technogologies.
  • Rapid intelligence. Briefing notes or informal conversations on breaking news and hot-take intelligence findings.
  • Analysis. Including scorecards, foresight, scenarios, horizon scanning, vulnerability prioritisation and policy recommendations.
  • Colloquys. Convening stakeholders and experts in fireside chats, workshops, and Delphis
  • Pithy easy-to-consume deliverables designed for seniors, backed up by readable depth reports
