About Cardinal Insights

Evidence-based clarity for confident decision making.

We focus on the implications of emerging technology for society.

Human intelligance is at the heart of our work. We turn information into actionable insight by harnessing original thought, deep topic knowledge, and real-world practical experience. We identify, engage, and challenge independent experts who can pierce the hype and get to the heart of the matter. We distil their expertise, combine it with futures-oriented analytical techniques, and make succinct actionable recommendations. This human intelligence is often unpublished and otherwise inaccessible. We prioritise intellectual honesty, even when the findings are difficult or provocative.

Partnershups built on trust.

We form partnerships with our customers, taking on their challenges as our own, acting as trusted advisors and critical friends. In addition to deep research we often often providing rapid conversational insights in response to breaking news and new research, clarifying complexity without losing nuance.

Our work.

While we are generalists at heart, we have extensive experience investigating deepfakes, misinformation, child safety online, and malicious applications of AI, as well as futures work on a wide variety of technologies and public safety threats.

Our work informs policy and operations for government departments, regulators, security & policing, and others.

We have developed a lean distributed approach shortens time to value and maximises value for money.
