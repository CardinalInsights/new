About Cardinal Insights

Human intelligence for confident decision making

Human intelligence is at the heart of our work. We identify, engage, and challenge independent experts who can pierce the hype and get to the core of the matter. We harness original thought, deep topic knowledge, and real-world practical experience, combining it with futures thinking to turn information into actionable insight and succinct recommendations. This human intelligence is often unpublished and otherwise inaccessible.

Partnerships built on intellectual honesty

We form partnerships with our customers, taking on their challenges as our own, acting as trusted advisors and critical friends. We prioritise intellectual honesty, even when findings are difficult or provocative. In addition to deep research, we interpret breaking news or new research with rapid conversational insights, clarifying complexity without losing nuance.

A focus on the implications of emerging technology

While we are generalists at heart, we have extensive experience investigating deepfakes, misinformation, child safety online, and malicious applications of AI, as well as horizon scanning for a wide variety of technologies and public safety threats. This work informs policy and operations for government departments, regulators, security & policing, and others.

Collaborative delivery

Cardinal Insights has developed a lean distributed approach that shortens time to value and maximises value for money. We provide expertise both direct and via the Accelerated Capability Environment (ACE), a Home Office capability within the Homeland Security Group. We also collaborate with specialist consultants and market intelligence partners. We are part of the Vivace community, and are JOSCAR registered.
